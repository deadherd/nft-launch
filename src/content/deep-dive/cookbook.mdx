### Let the fam cook.

Down here, earning respect takes more than just luck.

Every family keeps a cookbook: a ledger of rituals, risks, and real-world tasks.

Complete the recipes, unlock rewards for the current [season](./seasons/one).

Each finished recipe is more than an achievement - it's proof you showed up, stirred the pot, and lived to serve it hot.
